Major Research Projects and Innovations

  • AI-Driven Health Diagnostics: A collaborative project developing AI models to diagnose complex health conditions with greater accuracy and efficiency.
  • Renewable Energy Integration: A research initiative exploring sustainable energy solutions and grid integration for a cleaner future.
  • Genome Editing for Rare Diseases: Focused on developing gene-editing techniques to treat and potentially cure rare genetic disorders.
  • Autonomous Robotics for Disaster Relief: An interdisciplinary project creating autonomous robots to assist in natural disaster relief efforts.
